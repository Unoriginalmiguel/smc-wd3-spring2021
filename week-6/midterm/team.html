Team Established 2020

RUBI WORKS is being built by five innovative global leaders.

Leonel Aruajo – Founder

An engineer by accident, he landed in Disney Innovative Labs after graduating college. He was later recruited to work at NASA where he met Emma, there they had an idea that would later become RUBI WORKS.

Emma Washington – Founder

Emma only ever wanted to work in the space industry and she loved every minute of being part of NASA for six years. She brought in Leonel one day into a meeting, they talked for hours about what they wanted to do and what NASA couldn’t do for them. In that meeting, they identified the power of efficiency.

Aaron Cole – Co-Founder & CTO

Aaron jumped in to help Emma and Leonel create the first prototype of what is now RUBI WORKS. He built the ecosystem that allows RUBI WORKS to work so flawlessly and because of that the company owns four patents based on the work they collaborated in.

Khristina Lebedev – Partner & COO

Khristina was sought out by RUBI WORKS, she declined multiple times but it wasn't RUBI WORKS persistence that brought her into the team. Her company was one of the first to buy and test RUBI WORKS prototype, she was sold a week in to using the product herself. Now, she uses her operational skills to keep RUBI WORKS growing at the same speed it launched.

Veronica Larsen – Co-Founder & CFO

Veronica was brought in the day after Leonel and Emma agreed to create their own company. Her experience interning at the best VCs in Silicon Valley allowed her to identify how to get RUBI WORKS to scale. She is now in charge of the entire financial direction of the company.
